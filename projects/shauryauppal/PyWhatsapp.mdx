<Overview>

Pywhatsapp is used to Automate Whatsapp through Whatsapp web. We can add
number of contacts whom we want to send messages or Media attachments (like
Video or Images or PDFs). Selenium, Autoit and Schedule have been used one
from Automation and other for Scheduling messages.

</Overview>
