<Overview>

A tic-tac-toe app from scratch!
  
  ###Requirements:
  - Android Studio
  - JDK tool kit
  - Emulator or an android device
  
  ### How to run the game?
  1.  Just run the app on your machine (make sure you have all the requirements installed)
  2.  You can either connect your android device or use an android emulator
  3.  Once the app is finished building, you can disconnect your device
  4.  Now just sit back and play Tic-Tac-Toe on your android device.

</Overview>
